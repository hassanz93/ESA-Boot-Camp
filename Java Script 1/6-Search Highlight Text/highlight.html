National Distance Learning Week is November 8-13 and the School of Public Health is celebrating the innovative faculty and tenacious students and alumni that make up their online learning community. Michigan Public Health's // National Distance Learning Week recognizes the work of online master's faculty to deliver top quality public health education to online students and create a flexible, supportive, and innovative experience with the Master of Public Health degree. It also highlights the online master's students and alumni who dedicate themselves to public health study while they continue to work, raise families, and live full lives, to contribute to a healthier world for all.
